❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Busting skepticism before the trolls show up

🤔 Is this real?

Yes. VPT-101 is based on published science and real protocols. Every sequence, protocol, and result is verifiable and reproducible. We're not selling snake oil—we're building the future of medicine.

🛡️ What's the risk?

All protocols include safety measures and negative controls. We're following established lab safety protocols. Start small, test thoroughly, and always prioritize safety. This is medicine, not magic.

🤝 How can I help?

Fork the repo, run the protocols, share your results—good or bad. Every datapoint makes the next person's trial safer and faster. No experience required, just curiosity and care.

⚖️ What about regulations?

We provide FDA/EMA templates and regulatory guidance. But remember: regulations exist to protect people, not to prevent progress. We're working within the system to change the system.
